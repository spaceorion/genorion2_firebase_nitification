About Us


Creating a new future...

GenOrion


GenOrion is founded by three automation enthusiasts in 2020.
Working towards the automation of Home, Offices and Automobiles completely
based on Indian technology under the Make In India intiative.

We are a Delhi based company located in Dwarka, New Delhi,
with the aim to provide affordable automation products to our customers
with the multiple options available for customization.
